Italian Margherita Pizza

The fresh basil, mozzarella, and ripe tomatoes create a perfect harmony that's hard to resist.

Ingridents

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large tomato, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  2. Roll Dough: Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread Sauce: Evenly spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border.
  4. Add Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add Tomatoes: Arrange tomato slices on top of the cheese..
  6. Bake Pizza: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish: Scatter fresh basil leaves on top, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve: Slice and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!

  • Chicken Biryani

    Combination of tender chicken with fragrant spices and fluffy rice.

    Ingridents

    • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
    • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 2 onions, thinly sliced
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 3-4 green chilies, slit
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves, chopped
    • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder,red chilli powder, cumin seeds
    • 4-5 whole cloves,peppercorns
    • 2-3 bay leaves
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick
    • 4 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or vegetable oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices, refrigerate for 1 hour.
    2. Parboil basmati rice, drain and set aside.
    3. Saut√© onions and spices, add marinated chicken, tomatoes, and green chilies, cook until chicken is partially done.
    4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, sprinkle with cilantro, mint, and saffron milk (optional).
    5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until rice is tender and chicken is fully cooked.
    6. Fluff with a fork, serve hot with raita (yogurt dip).
