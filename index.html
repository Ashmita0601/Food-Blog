Italian Margherita Pizza

The fresh basil, mozzarella, and ripe tomatoes create a perfect harmony that's hard to resist.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large tomato, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  2. Roll Dough: Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread Sauce: Evenly spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border.
  4. Add Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add Tomatoes: Arrange tomato slices on top of the cheese..
  6. Bake Pizza: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish: Scatter fresh basil leaves on top, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve: Slice and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!

  • Chicken Biryani

    Combination of tender chicken with fragrant spices and fluffy rice.

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
    • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 2 onions, thinly sliced
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 3-4 green chilies, slit
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves, chopped
    • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp turmeric powder,red chilli powder, cumin seeds
    • 4-5 whole cloves,peppercorns
    • 2-3 bay leaves
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick
    • 4 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or vegetable oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices, refrigerate for 1 hour.
    2. Parboil basmati rice, drain and set aside.
    3. Saut√© onions and spices, add marinated chicken, tomatoes, and green chilies, cook until chicken is partially done.
    4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, sprinkle with cilantro, mint, and saffron milk (optional).
    5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until rice is tender and chicken is fully cooked.
    6. Fluff with a fork, serve hot with raita (yogurt dip).

  • Classic Pancake

    Fluffy pancake are delightful breakfast favorite that everyone loves.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tsp sugar and melted butter
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup buttermilk (or milk)
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract(optional)
    • Butter/Oil for cooking

    Instructions

    1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
    3. Saut√© onions and spices, add marinated chicken, tomatoes, and green chilies, cook until chicken is partially done.
    4. Combine: Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; the batter should be slightly lumpy.
    5. Heat Pan: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add a bit of butter or oil.
    6. Cook Pancakes: Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form and edges are set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes.
    7. Serve: Serve warm with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, berries, or powdered sugar.

    Tips:

    • Rest the batter for 5-10 minutes for fluffier pancakes.
    • Thin the batter with more milk if it's too thick.

    Strawberry Lemonade

    Erase your thirst with a Strawberry Lemonade drink.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
    • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
    • 3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
    • 4-5 cups cold water
    • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)
    • Ice cubes

    Instructions

    1. Prepare Strawberry Syrup: Blend strawberries with water until smooth, then strain to remove seeds.
    2. Mix Lemonade: Combine strawberry puree, lemon juice, and sugar in a pitcher until sugar dissolves.
    3. Add Water: Pour 4-5 cups of cold water into the pitcher, adjusting to taste.
    4. Serve: Fill glasses with ice, pour in strawberry lemonade, and optionally garnish with fresh mint leaves.
    5. Enjoy: Savor this sweet, tangy, and fruity drink!

    Tips:

    • For an extra twist, add a splash of sparkling water or club soda to the lemonade for some fizz.
    • Adjust the sugar level based on your preference or dietary needs.

    Peda

    Healthy and Sweet dessert of Asia.

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup milk powder
    • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
    • 2 tsp ghee (clarified butter)
    • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • 2 tablespoons milk (as needed)
    • Chopped nuts for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Mix Ingredients: Combine milk powder and sweetened condensed milk in a heated pan with ghee.
    2. Cook Mixture: Stir continuously over low heat until it thickens and forms a dough-like consistency.
    3. Add Flavor: Mix in cardamom powder for aromatic flavor.
    4. Shape Pedas: Allow mixture to cool slightly, then shape into small round discs using greased hands.
    5. Garnish (Optional): Press chopped nuts on top for decoration.
    6. Cool and Store: Let pedas cool completely at room temperature before storing in an airtight container.
    7. Serve: Enjoy these creamy and aromatic pedas as a delightful sweet treat!

    Tips:

    • Adjust the sweetness by varying the amount of sweetened condensed milk.
    • For a richer flavor, you can add a few drops of rose water or saffron-infused milk to the mixture.
